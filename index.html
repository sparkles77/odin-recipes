Oven Roasted Asparagus

Description

This roasted asparagus recipe is easy, customizable, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus
  • garlic
  • salt
  • pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Lemon Juice
  1. Preheat Oven to 425
  2. Chop woody ends off asparagus
  3. Mince fresh garlic
  4. Add asparagus to baking sheet and toss with olive olive and sprinkle it with garlic, cheese, salt, and pepper.
  5. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes, until tender
  6. Squeeze fresh lemon over asparagus and serve
